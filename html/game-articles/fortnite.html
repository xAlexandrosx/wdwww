Developer: Epic Games
Release Date: July 25, 2017
Genre: Battle Royale, Sandbox, Survival
Platform: PC, macOS, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, Android, iOS (limited)

Overview

Fortnite is a highly popular online multiplayer game developed by Epic Games. It is best known for its Battle Royale mode, where 100 players drop onto an island and fight to be the last one standing. With its fast-paced gameplay, vibrant visuals, and frequent updates, Fortnite has become a cultural phenomenon since its release.

Gameplay

The most popular mode, Fortnite: Battle Royale, combines third-person shooting, scavenging, and base-building mechanics. Players gather materials to build walls, ramps, and structures for both offense and defense. The shrinking storm circle forces players into tighter areas, encouraging constant movement and encounters.

Fortnite also offers a Creative Mode for sandbox-style building and custom games, and Save the World, a cooperative PvE mode where players fight off zombie-like creatures and defend objectives.

Seasons and Updates

Fortnite operates on a seasonal model, introducing new themes, map changes, cosmetics, and gameplay elements every few months. Crossovers with popular franchises such as Marvel, Star Wars, and anime like Dragon Ball have helped the game stay fresh and relevant.

Cosmetics and Battle Pass

Fortnite is free to play and monetized through cosmetic items like skins, emotes, and gliders. Players can purchase these directly or earn them through the Battle Pass, a seasonal progression system with exclusive rewards.
