Founded: 2005
Founders: Jamie Cheng, Phillipe Vachon
Headquarters: Vancouver, Canada
Industry: Video Game Development and Publishing

Overview

Klei Entertainment is an independent game studio celebrated for its unique blend of charming art styles, innovative gameplay, and deeply engaging player experiences. Founded in 2005, Klei has carved a niche by creating games that are not only challenging and creative but also filled with personality and heart.

Don’t Starve Together: A True Masterpiece

At the heart of Klei’s success is Don’t Starve Together, the beloved multiplayer expansion to the original Don’t Starve survival game. With its distinctive hand-drawn art, eerie yet whimsical atmosphere, and brutally rewarding gameplay, Don’t Starve Together invites players into a harsh wilderness where every day is a fight against hunger, madness, and mysterious creatures.

The cooperative nature of the game brings friends together to explore, craft, and survive in a world that constantly tests creativity and teamwork. Its regular content updates, seasonal events, and passionate community make it not just a game, but a living, breathing experience.

Other Noteworthy Titles

  • Mark of the Ninja: A stealth-action game praised for its tight controls and cinematic storytelling.
  • Oxygen Not Included: A complex colony simulator that challenges players to manage resources and keep their underground space colony alive.
  • Griftlands: A deck-building roguelike RPG with branching storylines and deep character interactions.

Philosophy and Craft

Klei Entertainment thrives on creating games with strong artistic vision, polished mechanics, and a touch of the unexpected. They embrace a player-centric approach, often engaging their community for feedback and ideas, which fuels ongoing improvements and expansions.

Their games invite players not just to play, but to think, experiment, and sometimes laugh at the unpredictable chaos that unfolds.

Community and Impact

Klei has fostered a welcoming and creative community, with Don’t Starve Together standing as a shining example of cooperative gameplay that encourages friendship and collaboration. Their commitment to supporting players through updates and events has earned them a loyal fanbase worldwide.

Conclusion

With a perfect blend of artistry, innovation, and heart, Klei Entertainment continues to craft experiences that resonate deeply with players. For anyone seeking games that challenge the mind and warm the soul, Klei’s catalog is a treasure trove waiting to be explored — especially if you love surviving the wild with friends in Don’t Starve Together.
