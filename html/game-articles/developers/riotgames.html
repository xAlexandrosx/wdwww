Founded: September 2006
Founders: Brandon Beck, Marc Merrill
Headquarters: Los Angeles, California, USA
Industry: Video Game Development and Publishing

Overview

Riot Games is a leading video game developer and publisher best known for creating the massively popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game League of Legends. Since its founding in 2006, Riot has grown into one of the most influential companies in the gaming industry, expanding its portfolio, supporting global esports, and producing media content beyond games.

Flagship Title: League of Legends

Riot Games launched League of Legends in 2009, and it quickly became a cornerstone of competitive online gaming. The game's accessibility, deep strategy, and frequent updates attracted millions of players worldwide, helping define the MOBA genre and setting a new standard for live-service games.

To this day, League of Legends remains one of the most played PC games globally and features a thriving professional esports scene with regional leagues and the annual World Championship.

Expanding the Universe

Building on the success of League of Legends, Riot expanded the universe of Runeterra through new game titles and storytelling formats:

  • Teamfight Tactics (TFT): An auto-battler based on League’s characters and universe.
  • Legends of Runeterra: A strategic collectible card game set in Runeterra.
  • Wild Rift: A mobile and console-optimized version of League of Legends.
  • Valorant: A competitive first-person shooter combining tactical gunplay with unique agent abilities.

Esports and Media

Riot Games has played a pivotal role in legitimizing esports on a global scale. The League of Legends World Championship draws millions of viewers and fills stadiums worldwide. Riot also supports professional Valorant competition through the Valorant Champions Tour.

Beyond games, Riot has produced high-quality media projects like the Emmy-winning animated series Arcane on Netflix, which explores the stories of League’s champions and expands the lore of the game’s universe.

Community and Culture

Riot emphasizes direct engagement with its global community through constant feedback, patch updates, and community events. The company has also made efforts to address internal culture issues and invest in inclusivity and player trust across all platforms.

Conclusion

From a single game studio to a multimedia powerhouse, Riot Games continues to shape the future of gaming and entertainment. With a strong focus on player experience, competitive play, and storytelling, Riot remains one of the most prominent forces in modern game development.
