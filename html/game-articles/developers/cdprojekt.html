Founded: May 1994
Founders: Marcin Iwiński, Michał Kiciński
Headquarters: Warsaw, Poland
Industry: Video Game Development and Publishing

Overview

CD Projekt is a prominent Polish video game developer and publisher, internationally recognized for its work on story-driven role-playing games. The company began as a distributor of foreign video games in Poland and later grew into one of the most acclaimed game developers in the world.

The Witcher Series

CD Projekt Red, the development division of CD Projekt, gained global fame through its adaptation of Andrzej Sapkowski's The Witcher novels. The franchise's standout title, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, released in 2015, became one of the most awarded games in history thanks to its vast open world, deep storytelling, and high production value.

The Witcher series has expanded into spin-offs, a Netflix show, and an upcoming remake of the original game, further establishing the brand’s cultural significance.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077, released in 2020, was one of the most anticipated games of the decade. Despite a rocky launch due to technical issues, especially on older consoles, the game was later improved through numerous updates. Its expansion, Phantom Liberty, and multiple patches helped restore much of the game’s reputation, showcasing CD Projekt’s long-term support philosophy.

GOG.com

CD Projekt also owns GOG.com (Good Old Games), a digital distribution platform that offers DRM-free titles. GOG has become known for preserving classic games while promoting user-friendly practices and fair pricing globally.

Development Philosophy

The studio emphasizes immersive single-player experiences, detailed worldbuilding, and player choice. Despite facing challenges, CD Projekt has maintained a strong reputation for storytelling and post-launch commitment, often providing large free updates and expansions for its games.

Future Projects

CD Projekt is currently working on multiple new titles, including a new Witcher trilogy, a sequel to Cyberpunk 2077 (codenamed Orion), and original IPs. The company is transitioning to Unreal Engine 5 to support these next-generation developments.

Conclusion

From its beginnings as a regional distributor to a globally influential studio, CD Projekt has become a symbol of ambitious, narrative-driven game design. With a passionate community and a commitment to improving its products, the company continues to shape the landscape of modern RPGs.
