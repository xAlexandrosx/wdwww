Developer: Warhorse Studios
Release Date: TBA 2024
Genre: Action RPG, Historical, Open World
Platform: PC, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S

Overview

Kingdom Come: Deliverance II is the sequel to the acclaimed historical RPG by Warhorse Studios. Set in 15th-century Bohemia during a time of civil unrest, the game continues the story of Henry, a blacksmith’s son turned warrior, as he navigates political intrigue, personal vengeance, and war-torn landscapes in pursuit of justice and identity.

Gameplay

Like its predecessor, Deliverance II emphasizes realism, immersion, and historically accurate detail. Players explore a vast open world filled with castles, towns, forests, and battlefields. The game features a deep narrative, reactive NPCs, and branching dialogue that impacts relationships and outcomes.

Combat remains grounded and skill-based, focusing on first-person melee with realistic swordplay, stamina management, and period-authentic weaponry. Players must eat, sleep, maintain gear, and manage reputation as they make choices in a world that reacts accordingly. Horse riding, archery, alchemy, and lockpicking all return with refinements and new mechanics.

Game Modes

  • Story Mode: A narrative-driven experience focused on Henry’s journey and the political unrest in Bohemia.
  • Free Exploration: Engage in side quests, explore the open world, and forge your path independently of the main storyline.
  • Hardcore Mode (Optional): A punishing mode with no HUD, realistic combat, limited saves, and greater survival demands.

Community and Modding

The original game developed a loyal modding community, and Deliverance II is expected to continue that trend. Players can anticipate quality-of-life tweaks, visual enhancements, and historical expansions. Warhorse Studios has expressed support for mod tools and community-created content post-launch.

Educational and Cultural Impact

Renowned for its dedication to historical accuracy, the Kingdom Come series provides an immersive look into medieval Central Europe, free of fantasy tropes. It's been praised for encouraging interest in history, linguistics, and medieval warfare. The sequel aims to deepen this by expanding the game world and continuing to portray real-world events and figures.

Conclusion

Kingdom Come: Deliverance II promises to deliver a richly detailed and authentic medieval RPG experience. With a compelling protagonist, moral complexity, and a meticulously crafted world, it stands as a unique offering for fans of grounded historical adventures and deep role-playing mechanics.
