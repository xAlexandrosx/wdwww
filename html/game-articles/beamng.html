Developer: BeamNG GmbH
Release Date: August 29, 2015 (Early Access)
Platform: PC (Windows)
Genre: Vehicle Simulation, Soft-body Physics

Overview

BeamNG.drive is a highly realistic vehicle simulation game developed by BeamNG GmbH, known for its advanced soft-body physics engine that provides incredibly detailed and dynamic vehicle damage and deformation. The game offers a sandbox experience where players can drive a wide variety of vehicles across diverse maps with authentic physics interactions.

Gameplay

BeamNG.drive allows players to test, drive, and crash vehicles in a highly detailed, physics-driven environment. The core of the experience is the soft-body physics system, which simulates the bending, twisting, and breaking of vehicles in real time, creating realistic crashes and mechanical failures.

Players can explore expansive open-world maps, participate in scenarios like races, challenges, or custom missions, and modify vehicles with a robust customization system. The game also supports modding, allowing the community to create new vehicles, maps, and gameplay modes.

Features

  • Advanced soft-body physics for realistic vehicle damage and handling.
  • Wide selection of vehicles ranging from compact cars to trucks and off-roaders.
  • Open-world maps with varied terrains, including urban environments, mountains, and deserts.
  • Custom scenarios, challenges, and multiplayer support.
  • Strong modding community with extensive user-created content.

Community and Development

BeamNG.drive benefits from an active and passionate community that contributes mods, tutorials, and gameplay videos. The developers continuously update the game, improving physics, adding vehicles, and expanding the world based on community feedback.
